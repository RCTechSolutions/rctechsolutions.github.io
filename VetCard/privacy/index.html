VetCard - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Overview

VetCard is a pet health management app designed to help users organize information about their pets, including vaccines, appointments, medicines, nutrition plans, weight records, notes, photos, and PDF reports.

2. Information Stored by the App

VetCard may store the following information entered by the user:

  • Owner profile information, such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Pet information, such as name, species, breed, gender, birthday, color, size, and photos.
  • Health-related pet records, including vaccines, medicines, appointments, nutrition plans, weight records, notes, and reminders.

3. How Information Is Stored

VetCard stores data locally on the user's device. If iCloud is enabled on the user's device, app data may also be synchronized through Apple's iCloud services using the user's Apple account.

4. Data Sharing

VetCard does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. iCloud

If iCloud synchronization is available and enabled, data may be stored and synced through Apple's iCloud infrastructure. iCloud data is handled according to Apple's privacy and security practices.

6. Photos and PDF Reports

Users may add photos of pets, vaccines, medicines, or notes. Users may also generate PDF reports from the information stored in the app. These files remain under the user's control and are only shared when the user chooses to export or share them.

7. Notifications

VetCard may request permission to send local notifications for reminders such as appointments, medicines, vaccines, and nutrition schedules. Users can enable or disable notifications at any time from the app settings or system settings.

8. Children's Privacy

VetCard is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed for general pet care organization by users who manage their own device and iCloud account.

9. Data Deletion

Users can delete pets, records, notes, photos, and other stored information from within the app. Users may also remove app data by deleting the app and managing their iCloud storage settings if iCloud sync is used.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
